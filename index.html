Our Wellness Programs

Hormonal Wellness

Single focus: to optimize your hormones.

This program is for people with symptoms of hormonal imbalance: hot flashes, mood swings, night sweats, fatigue, insomnia, low libido, anxiety, irritability, depression, brain fog. We will focus exclusively on your hormones.

Labs included:

  • Stress hormones: cortisol, DHEA
  • Complete thyroid panel
  • Sex hormones (estrogen, progesterone, testosterone)
  • Vit D level

We will meet for the initial evaluation, then for the review of findings a month later, then again in 3 months. In addition, you will check in with us monthly to ensure improvement or resolution of your symptoms. We will repeat some labs prior to the end of the program. At the end of 4 month period we will do a comprehensive review of your progress, recommendations on supplements and doses of your bioidentical hormones if applicable.

Executive Wellness

Focus: prevention and maximized performance. I want the next level!

This program is for people who are healthy and well, and already have good lifestyle habits. You eat well, exercise regularly, have normal weight, and you want to make sure you continue doing well in the future. You are interested in preventive care, and/or bioidentical hormones when applicable.

Labs MAY include (we will choose based on complete medical history and symptoms):

  • Comprehensive digestive analysis (digestion, absorption, microbes, overgrowth, yeast)
  • Breath test to exclude bacterial ovregrowth
  • Evaluation of nutritional status
  • Kidney and liver function
  • Electrolytes
  • Blood Cell count
  • Vit D
  • Vit B12
  • Stress Hormones (cortisol, DHEA)
  • Complete thyroid panel
  • Sex hormones (estrogen, progesterone, testosterone)
  • Estrogen metabolism for cancer risk
  • Food sensitivities
  • Metabolism (blood sugar regulation, fasting insulin)
  • Inflammatory markers
  • Urine bone breakdown indicating risk for osteoporosis

We will meet for comprehensive evaluation of your history, symptoms, and goals. Once your tests are completed, we will meet to review findings and develop a comprehensive personalized plan to optimize your health. You will be started on personalized supplement regimen, and we will start bioidentical hormone replacement if appropriate and this is one of your goals. During your month program we will see you monthly. At the end of the program we will repeat hormone levels to make sure you are making appropriate progress. Your final in-depth appointment will go over long term recommendations for you to stay well.

Optimal Wellness

Focus: to get you back to feeling like you again

This program is for people who already have some health issues, like extra weight, preDM or diabetes, hypertension, arthritis, autoimmunity, and high cholesterol. These people suffer with multiple symptoms and often say they don’t feel like themselves anymore. This 6 month program is for people who are committed to feel better and do what it takes, and need a mentor or an expert who can guide them through the right steps. We use comprehensive approach optimizing your nutrition, helping to process toxins, modifying lifestyle habits, treating deficiencies, developing resiliency and providing health education.

Labs MAY include (we will choose based on complete medical history and symptoms):

  • Evaluation of gut health (digestion, absorption, microbes, overgrowth, yeast)
  • Evaluation of nutritional status
  • Kidney and liver function
  • Vit D
  • Vit B12
  • Stress Hormones (cortisol, DHEA)
  • Complete thyroid panel
  • Sex hormones (estrogen, progesterone, testosterone)
  • Estrogen metabolism for cancer risk
  • Food sensitivities
  • Metabolism (blood sugar regulation, fasting insulin)
  • Inflammatory markers

We will meet for comprehensive evaluation of your history, symptoms, and goals. Once your tests are completed, we will meet to review findings and develop a comprehensive personalized plan to restore your health. During your 6 month program you will remain on personalized supplement regimen, receive health coaching, and have regular access to our team. We will see you monthly. Ongoing monitoring will help us address more issues at the same time and modify your program as needed, so you can experience results sooner. At the end of your program we may repeat some tests if necessary to make sure you are making appropriate progress. Your final in-depth appointment will go over long term recommendations for you to stay well.
